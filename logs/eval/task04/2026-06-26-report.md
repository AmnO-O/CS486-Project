### Key improvement: Defense-in-depth for BR8/BR9

Originally, check-in time and condition recording (BR8/BR9) were documented as application-level responsibilities. During re-validation, the team determined that database-level triggers provided stronger enforcement. Two triggers were added:

- `trg_bookings_checkin_enforcement` — enforces `actual_start_time` and `initial_condition` NOT NULL when status transitions to `checked_in`
- `trg_bookings_completion_enforcement` — enforces `actual_end_time` and `final_condition` NOT NULL when status transitions to `completed`

This ensured that no application bug could bypass these requirements.
